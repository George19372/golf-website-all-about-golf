Welcome to All About Golf

This website provides detailed information on the essential golf equipment you need to play the game. From drivers to putters, discover how each club functions and learn about various golf accessories that enhance your performance on the course.

A Driver is used for long-distance shots off the tee. It has a large head and long shaft to help launch the ball far down the fairway.

Hybrids combine features of irons and woods. They're great for distance and control, especially for beginners.

Irons are used for accurate shots toward the green. Lower numbers hit farther, higher numbers go higher with less distance.

Putters are used on the green to roll the ball into the hole. They come in different styles to match putting preferences.

Golf Accessories:

  • Tees – Hold the ball for the first shot.
  • Ball Markers – Mark your spot on the green.
  • Gloves – Improve grip and comfort.
  • Towels – Keep clubs and hands clean.
