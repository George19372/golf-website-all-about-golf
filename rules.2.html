Golf Etiquette

  • Being Quiet

    When someone is about to hit, be quiet to help them focus on their best shot.

  • Pace of Play

    Play at a good pace to avoid holding up others. An 18-hole round should take about 3.5 – 4 hours.

  • Bunkers

    Always rake bunkers after use.

  • Divots

    Replace turf you take out with your shots.

  • Pitch Marks

    Repair pitch marks on the green.

  • Letting Faster Groups Play Through

    Let faster groups go ahead if you’re slower.

  • Safety

    Yell "Fore!" if your ball might hit someone.

  • Cell Phones and Music

    Keep phones silent and music low.
